Web Development

I am not your typical web developer.
Being able to create something, seeing my visions come to life and feeling that sense of accomplishment is extremely beautiful.

Following my path had me face-to-face with some detours. Studying Business Administration at the University of Applied Sciences in Kempten (Bavaria/Germany), I was actually planning to follow a different route. Working for the dean of the computer science department at the university and then later for a company that specialized in an enterprise resource planning system made me question my choice of career.

Finally I decided to embark in the journey of becoming a web developer. From using HTML5 and CSS3 to master JavaScript, I am self-thought and spend a lot of time perfecting my skills and working on certificates.
